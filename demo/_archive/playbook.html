Project Assessment

Initial Call
1-2 people // 20 minutes

Call with your project stakeholders to discuss the project and determine if it is a good fit for our process.

1. Discovery Phase

The first step in any new engagement is a short meeting to discuss your business, your goals, and what success looks like for the project.

1.1
Phase Kickoff Meeting
4-6 people // 60-90 minutes

Meeting between our team and your critical stakeholders to discuss the project goals, process, and timeline.

1.2
Current Product Review (optional)
2-4 people // 1-3 weeks

Series of meetings with your team to document the current state of the product and the ecosystem it exists in.

Activities include:
1.2.1 — Site or App Definition
1.2.2 — UI / Usability Assessment
1.2.3 — Landscape Analysis
1.2.4 — Competitive GAP Analysis
1.2.5 — Research Synthesis
1.2.6 — Internal Review & Approval
1.2.7 — Executive Summary Presentation
1.3
Interviews
2-4 people // 1-3 weeks

Series of meetings with your team to document the current state of the product and the ecosystem it exists in.

Activities include:
1.3.1 — Interview Script Preparation

1.3.3 — Partner Interviews
1.3.4 — Customer Interviews
1.3.5 — Research Synthesis
1.3.6 — Executive Summary Presentation
1.4
Target Market Definition
2-4 people // 1-3 weeks
